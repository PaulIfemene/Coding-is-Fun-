The Moon Project is inspired by the first moon landing,
the astronauts marked their visit with the US flag.
Similarly, this project helps tech event attendees by collecting their information and printing a real-time badge to prove their attendance,
just like a "stamp" on the moon. It's a digital testament to participating in tech events,
making each attendee’s involvement official and memorable.
